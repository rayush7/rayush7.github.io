I am a Graduate Student at Ecole CentraleSupelec, University of Paris-Saclay, Paris pursuing M.Sc in Artificial Intelligence.

Prior to this, I was working at StegoSOC (Formerly Cloudadic, a US based AI Startup  in CyberSecurity) as Deep Learning Engineer in Bangalore, India. Previously I had also been engaged in IIITD‘s project on Self Driving Cars for Indian Roads (part of Spark The Rise : Driver-less Car Challenge by Mahindra) as Research Associate and was also employed at an AI based startup Cube26 (Acquired by Paytm) as Data Science and Machine Learning intern.

I firmly hold the opinion that the true essence of engineering is to solve real life and challenging problems to make life simple and undemanding for the society. Intrigued by Artificial Intelligence, I am very curious to explore various areas where AI based models can be deployed and thereby influence the lives of common masses in a constructive fashion. I am interested working in the field of data science, applied machine learning, computer vision, natural language processing, robotics, deep learning and any other subfield of artificial intelligence.

I prefer working in Research Labs to work with people who are driven by passion, dignity and vigor. Currently I am looking for Master’s Thesis (Research) starting April/May 2019.
